Electric Motors

At FDAP Engineering, we offer a wide range of electric motors to suit various industrial applications:

AC motors : are commonly used in manufacturing and machinery due to their durability and efficiency; they include induction motors, which are reliable and low-maintenance, and synchronous motors, ideal for applications requiring constant speed.

 DC motors : provide excellent speed control and are often used in applications like conveyors and cranes; they come in brushed or brushless versions, with brushless motors offering longer life and less maintenance. Servo motors are designed for precision control and are widely used in robotics, automation, and CNC machinery. 

Stepper motors : move in fixed steps and are perfect for applications that require accurate positioning, such as 3D printers and packaging machines. We also supply universal motors, which can run on both AC and DC power and are typically used in portable tools and appliances. Whatever your need, FDAP Engineering has the right motor solution for your operation.
